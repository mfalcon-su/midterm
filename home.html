WE BELIEVE

that the key to a successful life is more important than just

healthy eating and looking good. It’s about feeling the best you

can while doing your best for the life around you. It’s about

having strong relationships with the people surrounding you and

the worlds both inside and out. It means treating animals,

Mother Nature, and our bodies with the respect they deserve.

It means being conscious about what we consume, how our daily

choices affect the Earth, and how, with a clear mind, we can grow

and become one with the boss ladies inside us all.

We are striving for a sustainable future through food,

compassion, community, shared ideas, and non conformity.
